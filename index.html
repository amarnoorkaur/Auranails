Our Story

More Than a
Nail Studio

At Aura Nails Studio, we believe every set of nails tells a story. Nestled in the heart of Surrey, BC, our boutique studio is a sanctuary of beauty, creativity, and self-care.

Our master nail technicians are trained in the latest techniques — from delicate Korean nail art to bold sculptural acrylics. We use only premium, non-toxic products that are as kind to your nails as they are beautiful.

When you step into Aura, you're not just getting a nail appointment. You're stepping into a moment just for you.
